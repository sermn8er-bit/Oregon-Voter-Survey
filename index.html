# Oregon-Voter-Survey Oregon Voter Survey - Privacy & Terms

Privacy Policy

This survey collects limited personal information including mobile phone number, general geographic information, and survey responses.

This information is used solely for research and analysis of public opinion and is not used for marketing, sales, or fundraising.

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties. Data may be analyzed in aggregate form for reporting purposes.

SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates.

Participation is voluntary. Recipients may opt out at any time by replying STOP.

SMS Terms of Service

By participating in this survey, you agree to receive SMS messages inviting you to participate in a voluntary voter opinion survey.

Message frequency may vary.

Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out at any time, reply STOP.

For assistance, reply HELP.
